== PRAJWAL WEDDING — MEDIA ASSETS ==

Drop the following files into this folder once finalised:

  video.mp4       — Invite video (plays on the invite page)
  music.mp3       — Background music (loops on the invite page)
  poster.webp     — Video poster / still frame (shown while video loads)
  texture.webp    — Subtle paper texture overlay (optional, used in envelope screen)
  preview.jpg     — OG / social share preview image (1200 x 630 px recommended)

After adding files, update the paths in site-config.js → events[0].media
